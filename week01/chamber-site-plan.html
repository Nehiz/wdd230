Benin Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Planning a website to folster community growth and local business networking

Site Name

Benin Chamber of Commerce was selected as the name for this project. This name refelcts the locale of Benin Chamber of Commerce, a vibrant and growing community with a thriving business culture. The word "Chamber" emphasizes the purpose of the site: to promote local business interests, attract tourism, and advocate for community growth.

A potential domain name for the site could be beninchamber.org, which is professional and aligns with standard chamber of commerce domain practices.

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about Benin City, its history, and its vibrant local business community. It will promote local businesses, events, and attractions to encourage economic growth and tourism. The site will also serve as a tool for community networking, allowing businesses to join the chamber and access valuable resources.

Scenarios

The site content will address the needs of various target audiences. Example scenarios include:

  • What are the upcoming business-to-business networking events?
  • What resources or support does the chamber provide for new business starting in Benin?
  • How can I apply for membership, and what benefits do members of the Benin Chamber of Commerce receive?

Color Schema

The site will use a clean and professional color palette:

  • #0078A0 (Blue): Used for headings, navigation, and key accents to symbolize trust and professionalism.
  • #F4F4F4 (Light Gray): Background color for the site to ensure readability and contrast.

Typography

The site will use the following fonts:

  • Roboto: For body text, ensuring readability and modern design.
  • Montserrat: For headings to create a bold and professional appearance.
